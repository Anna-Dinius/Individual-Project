About NomNom Safe

NomNom Safe is a mobile application designed to help individuals with food allergies or dietary restrictions find compatible menu items at local restaurants.

This application addresses the problem of navigating food allergies and other dietary restrictions when dining out. Users can select specific foods to avoid and set a location radius to filter restaurants accordingly.

NomNom Safe empowers users to dine out with confidence, knowing their dietary needs are respected and supported.

App Features

Allergen Selection

Users can choose from common allergens like milk, eggs, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, and peanuts.

Restaurant Matching

The app displays restaurants that accommodate the selected dietary restrictions for safer dining experiences.

Menu Filtering

Users can view and filter each restaurant's menu items to find exactly what they're looking for.
